Latest College Policies on Covid Controlling

In the event of an influenza pandemic, colleges and universities will play an integral role in protecting the health and safety of students, employees and their families. Colleges across the states are taking active control over the spread of the pandemic. Policies change all the time and it is important that you as a college student know what to do next. You may check out the latest policies and other related topics by typing in your school name and clicking on the topic buttons.
